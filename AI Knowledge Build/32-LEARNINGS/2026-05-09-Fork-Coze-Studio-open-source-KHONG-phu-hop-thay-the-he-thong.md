# Fork Coze Studio open-source KHONG phu hop thay the he thong co san

## Boi canh
User muon dung Coze Studio (ByteDance, Apache 2.0) de cho khach hang custom AI agent. Da research chi tiet repo github.com/coze-dev/coze-studio.

## Giai phap
REJECT fork Coze Studio vi: 1) Ban open-source DA CAT BO tat ca messaging channels (Telegram, FB, Slack — chi co tren coze.com SaaS). 2) Backend Go (he thong hien tai Node.js). 3) Can 11 Docker services (MySQL, Redis, ES, MinIO, etcd, Milvus, NSQ x3, server, web). 4) Pre-1.0 (v0.5.1), 502 issues mo, tai lieu thieu. 5) Phai viet lai toan bo integration tu dau.

## Duc ket
Khi danh gia fork open-source platform: 1) Kiem tra ban OSS co day du feature nhu ban cloud/SaaS khong (thuong bi cat). 2) Kiem tra tech stack co compatible khong. 3) Dem so services can deploy. 4) Doc issues/maturity truoc khi quyet dinh. Coze OSS thieu messaging = mat 50% gia tri.
