# Newsletter RSS 2026 — Beehiiv format rss.beehiiv.com/feeds/ID.xml, Substack /feed

## Boi canh
Research newsletter RSS feeds. Nhiều newsletter dùng custom domain nhưng underlying platform có RSS riêng. Beehiiv RSS format khác: rss.beehiiv.com/feeds/{UNIQUE_ID}.xml. Substack luôn có /feed endpoint.

## Giai phap
The Rundown AI (Beehiiv): rss.beehiiv.com/feeds/2R3C6Bt5wj.xml. Ben's Bites chuyển sang Substack: bensbites.substack.com/feed. Import AI: importai.substack.com/feed. TLDR AI không có RSS, dùng archive scraping. feedparser v6+ vẫn gold standard.

## Duc ket
Newsletter RSS 2026: Substack = {name}.substack.com/feed luôn có. Beehiiv = rss.beehiiv.com/feeds/{ID}.xml. Custom domain có thể /feed không hoạt động — tìm underlying platform RSS thay thế.
